About NIKHIL Group

NIKHIL Group is a nationwide network of compassionate individuals united by the common goal of serving humanity and preserving our cultural heritage.

Founded in 2015, we've grown from a small group of 5 friends to a movement of over 12,500 members across India. Our name "NIKHIL" represents our vision - "Noble Initiatives for Kindness, Humanity, and Indian Legacy".

What began as local relief efforts during the Kerala floods has now expanded into a multi-faceted organization working in disaster response, animal welfare, and cultural preservation.
